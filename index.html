Steph NFT

StephNFT is an NFT Minting Platform built with JavaScript, Rarible Plugin, Moralis SDK and Web3.js ,
It takes into consideration that a lot of artists would love to create their own NFTs but can't afford to hence why this platform lets you mint your NFTs for Zero Gas Fees.

Disclaimer: This version only deploys NFTs to the Rinkeby Testnet.
